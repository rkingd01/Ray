Stalin's Purges

State Propaganda

The purges or reforms were preceded by an intense programme of propaganda inciting hatred toward landowners, successful farmers, non-Russians and church-goers as 'enemies of the people'.

Evictions

The reforms were preceded by an intense programme of propaganda inciting hatred toward landowners, successful farmers, non-Russians and church-goers as 'enemies of the people' or 'Kulaks'. Dekulakisation was introduced to cleanse the counry and this began with dispossessing the accused.

Orphans

It is estimated that more than 10 million people were either executed or deported. Without parents thousands of children were sent to orphanages.
