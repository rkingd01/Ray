Stalin's Purges

State Propaganda

The earliest of Stalin's purges (1929-32) was preceded by an intense programme of propaganda inciting hatred and fueling paranoia toward land-owning farmers, non-Russians and church-goers as 'enemies of the people' or Kulaks. Stalin announced the "liquidation of the kulaks as a class" on 27 December 1929. Anti-Kulak posters became commonplace with a strong visual impact in order to reach the illiterate.
