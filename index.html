Albert Georgijev

My father was known simply as 'Fred'. Only his closest and dearest knew that he originated from Ingria, Russia although he considered Estonia to be his home country. Even fewer knew that his real name was 'Albert'. A deep sadness in his past he kept to himself. A past where his family were stripped of their farm, evicted and made homeless in Stalin's Russia. However, nothing compared to the sadness of his mother dying after getting her children to a safe place.
