Albert Georgijev - UNDER CONSTRUCTION

My father's many friends knew him simply as 'Fred'. They had little idea that he originated from Ingria, Russia or that his real name was 'Albert'. The sadness in his past he tended to keep to himself: the sadness of his family being stripped of their farm, evicted, and left homeless in Stalin's Russia. Worst of all the sadness of his mother Alide passing in order to keep her children safe.
