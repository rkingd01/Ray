Albert Georgijev

My father was known by most as 'Fred'. 'Alfred' according to his British passport. However, his birth name was 'Albert' and he was born and raised in Niitskovitsa village, Ingria, Russia. However, he considered Estonia to be home.
A deep sadness in his past he kept to himself. A past where his family were stripped of all they owned and made homeless in Stalin's Russia.
However, nothing compared to the sadness of his mother dying after getting her children to a safe place.
